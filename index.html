Legal entailment: a self-test

Each item is a real question from the Japanese Bar Examination, as used in the COLIEE 2026 evaluation. Read the statutory provision and the statement, then decide whether the statement is entailed by the provision.

These items turn on precise reading of statutory exceptions and definitions. For each one, the answer returned by the system I developed for COLIEE 2026 — built entirely from open models — is shown alongside the correct answer. The system placed first internationally on this task.
